my orchids

I own a few dozen orchids, most inherited from my grandma. They're surprisingly easy to care for, and I love them very much.

I'll reorganize this page eventually; it's mainly just pretty photos for now.

cattleya

These grow as a series of vertical bulbs with leaves and beautiful ruffled flowers on top. I've duplicated mine by cutting between the stalks while repotting.

brassavola (?)

I'm not sure if this is a brassavola, brassocattleya, or something else. Interestingly, the petals fade from magenta to white over a few weeks. It grows much like the cattleyas and was easy to propagate as well.

dendrobium

Another sympodial orchid. Unlike the others, it forms cute baby plants (complete with roots!) midair. Mine has very pretty but short-lived flowers.

phalaenopsis

These have a central crown with flat leaves, long stems and a huge variety of flower patterns. I don't know how to duplicate them yet.

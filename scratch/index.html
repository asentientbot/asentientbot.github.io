SB2 to SWF converter

Back in 2015, I made a tool to bundle Scratch projects with a modified copy of the editor, letting them be shared as standalone Flash games. Much like Scratch 2 and Flash themselves, this is completely obsolete, but similar projects like HTMLifier are available for Scratch 3.

Thank you to everyone who sent encouraging feedback; as my first "real" coding project, it meant a lot to me.

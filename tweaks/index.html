jailbreak tweaks

I wrote a few simple tweaks when I used an iPhone 4S. Since I have an iPhone SE now, these are very unlikely to be updated.

iOS 6 AirPods hack

This tweak tries to fix connection issues by looping a silent MP3; it does help but I don't really understand why. Reddit post.

subcritical

A workaround for random shutdowns in cold weather. This may degrade your phone's battery or cause data loss. Source code.

shy controls

It hides the iOS 9 lock screen music controls until the home button is clicked, the opposite of the default behavior. Source code.

iBooks volume keys

This tweak enables the volume keys to turn pages, an FBReader feature I missed from my Moto E4.
